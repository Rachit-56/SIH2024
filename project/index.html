About the Benaras Hindu University Museum

The Benaras Hindu University Museum is a renowned institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich cultural heritage of India. Established in 1920, the museum houses a vast collection of artifacts, including ancient sculptures, paintings, manuscripts, textiles, and archaeological finds.

Our mission is to inspire and educate visitors through our diverse exhibits, educational programs, and research initiatives. We strive to foster a deeper understanding of India's history, traditions, and artistic achievements.
